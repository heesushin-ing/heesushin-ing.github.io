Suites of BtoB services in finance

Rethinking complexity•at Societe Generale CIB (2017 - Now)

From daily tools for internal staff to professional financial services for sales, analysts, syndicate originators, etc, I have been working on many different projects as a UX lead. Also, our team has designed and developed our SG Design System to unite the user experience across the services.

The UX designer's role in Societe Generale CIB is to simplify what is complicated, but to leave the right level of business complexity to the expert user. For over 4.5 years, I have been involved in 10+ projects and performed the role. If you've got a password from me, you can check my three short-listed case studies which describe my projects briefly.
