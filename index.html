As a UX/UI designer in Societe Generale CIB, currently based in London, I am tackling complex financial projects in the BtoB space. That's why our team motto is 'Rethink Complexity'.
From daily tools for internal staff to professional financial services for sales, analysts, syndicate originators, etc, I have been working on many different projects. Also, our team has designed and developed our SG design system to unite the user experience across the services.

Even 10 years ago, most of the concept cars from industry were only emphasising how fast and sexy they are. However, the trend is completely changed. The vehicle is not just a transportation, but it is the space closed related to our lives.
Thus, crafting the user experience within and even around the mobility is becoming more and more important. Although I work in finance industry currently, I have been interested in vehicle. These are my side projects, collaborating with a vehicle designer, Dain Kim.

2016
HOO was 100% my personal work from concept development, product design to UX/UI/GUI design: 360 Degree Airwasher with iPhone/iWatch applications.
